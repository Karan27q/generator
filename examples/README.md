# Example Prompts

Use these prompts to demo each tab in AI Creative Studio.

## Text → Image

- A futuristic city at sunset with flying cars and neon billboards
- A cozy cottage in an enchanted forest, fantasy art style
- Portrait of a robot barista in a cyberpunk café

## Image → Image

Upload any photo and try:

- Turn this into an oil painting with textured brushstrokes
- Make it look like a Studio Ghibli anime scene
- Convert to a pencil sketch, monochrome

## Text → Video

- A golden retriever running through a field of flowers in slow motion
- Ocean waves crashing on a rocky shore at golden hour
- A spaceship launching from a futuristic spaceport

## Image → Video

Upload a landscape or portrait and try:

- Gentle camera pan with clouds moving in the sky
- Subtle wind blowing through hair and leaves
- Slow zoom in with cinematic lighting
